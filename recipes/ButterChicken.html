Butter Chicken Recipe

This recipe provides a guide to making rich and creamy Butter Chicken. It's a popular Indian dish featuring tender chicken in a spiced tomato and cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, marinate the chicken with yogurt, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter and saut√© the garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the heavy cream, salt, and cook for another 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
