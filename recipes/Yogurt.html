Strawberry Panna Cotta Recipe

A classic Italian dessert, this Strawberry Panna Cotta is silky smooth and topped with fresh strawberry sauce, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 packet unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, pureed
  • Extra strawberries for garnish

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, sprinkle the gelatin over cold water and let it sit for 5 minutes to bloom.
  2. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream, milk, and sugar over medium heat until the sugar dissolves. Do not let it boil.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and bloomed gelatin until fully dissolved.
  4. Pour the mixture into serving glasses or molds and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  5. Once set, top with fresh strawberry puree and garnish with extra strawberries.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy!
